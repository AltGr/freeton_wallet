About
=====

The :code:`ft` tool is a multi-account multi-network terminal wallet
for users and developers on FreeTON.

Main features are:

* Easily manage multiple accounts (associations between symbolic names and keys, public or secret)
* Easily manage different sets of accounts for different networks (mainnet, testnet, rustnet, etc.)
* Easily spawn a local sandbox network for testing (using TONOS SE)
* Easily compile, deploy, call and watch Solidity smart contracts
* Easily inspect the blockchain state (head, blocks, transactions, messages)
